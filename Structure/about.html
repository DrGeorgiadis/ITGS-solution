About Us

The Epistemology Collective is a student-led project dedicated to educating adolescents on logical reasoning, critical thinking and the intersections between philosophy and science

Our Mission

To educate and inspire adolescents to develop a lifelong relationship with philosophy and science

Our Vision

To create a community of curious and critical thinkers that engage with the world around them and ask thought-provoking questions about the nature of knowledge, truth, and reality

Our Values

  • Curiosity
  • Creativity
  • Community
  • Diversity
  • Respect
