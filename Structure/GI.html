Get Involved

We are looking for intellectually curious and driven individuals with a passion for science and philosophy, and the will to disseminate that passion onto others

Example Activities

  • • Creating YouTube videos
  • • Internet Blogs
  • • Writing & Publishing Books
  • • Organizing debates
  • • Organizing Podcasts with guest speakers

Steps to apply

  • • Contact us through email or phone to express will to apply
  • • Write a personal statement explaining your passion about science & philosophy, as well as how your profile aligns with our values
  • • Send us the personal statement through email
  • • Wait for an email scheduling an interview
  • • After the interview, we will contact you with a decision on your application
